Statistics calculation is another part of deriving business value using data.
We enrich the data using categories to better visualize this, but this sparse data is not usefull yet.
Business generally dont make a very coarse grained analysis, but they need to better utilize time, so, we need summarizations to extract insight.
That way, we take a good amount of data and make the summations, extract the medium, median, count and any other summarizations that should be usefull to business.
