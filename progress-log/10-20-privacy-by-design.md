One of the processes that are very important in data engineering (and in most outsourcing job integration) is data anonimization.
Its very important to anonimize sensible data following principles of least privilege in security. Normally, things like name, user id, birth date aren't necessary in a average invoice
analysis for the BI team. 
In some cases, the choice of anonimization algorithm is very important to preserve joinability, where we can track some user habits or historical data for a single entity.
